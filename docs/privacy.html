Privacy Policy (LitSpark)

Effective date: 2025-12-28

LitSpark (“the app”) is a lightweight, offline-first app that shows short literary excerpts and lets you share them. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled when you use the app.

Summary

  • No account, no signup.
  • Core content is offline (bundled with the app).
  • Small local-only data for streaks and ad frequency.
  • Ads (mobile only): uses Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) on Android/iOS.

Information we collect

Information you provide

We do not ask you to provide personal information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or contacts) to use the app.

Information stored on your device (local storage)

The app stores small pieces of data only on your device, such as:

  • Your current reading streak (a number)
  • The last day the app was opened (a day key)
  • The last day an interstitial ad was shown (a day key)

This information is used solely to provide app features and to limit ad frequency.

Advertising data (Google AdMob)

If you use the app on Android or iOS, the app may show ads via Google AdMob. Google may collect device and usage information to deliver and measure ads.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Advertising: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Information we share

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties except as needed to provide ads on mobile platforms through Google AdMob.

Sharing features

When you tap Share, the app uses your device’s system share sheet to send text or an image to another app you choose.

Children’s privacy

LitSpark is a general-audience app and is not intended to be directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you choose to list the app as targeting children under 13, you may need to configure your advertising and app settings to comply with COPPA and Google Play’s policies.

Data retention

Local streak/ad-gate data remains on your device until you clear the app’s storage or uninstall the app. Advertising-related data retention is governed by Google’s policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date when changes are made.
